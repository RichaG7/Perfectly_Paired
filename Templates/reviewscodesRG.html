Objective

To predict wine varieties by their country, province, points (80-100), category (red or white), and descriptors using Random Forest Model.

The Approach

We employed two approaches with the Wine Reviews dataset - textual analysis and machine learning after quantification of the text. In the Random Rorest model, we used the frequency of descriptors used in reviews to classify wines.

We knew from the psychochemical dataset that the accuracy for predicting wine variety was not very good, and with the descriptors model the closest I came to was around 58%. The accuracy for predicting wine category (red or white) was much higher (99.9%), but that prediction was less useful.

Therefore, we employed the variety classification model nonetheless.
