Model Summary

Models:
Of the models used - Multinomial NB, Complement NB, Deep Learning - Deep Learning performed the best due to the complexity of the dataset.

Challenges:
Because of the complexity and size of data, deep learning worked very slowly. That’s why we could not test as many variations as we would have liked in order to find the best parameters. We could have used hyperparameter tuning to find the highest accuracy.

Assumptions:
We trained the model based on wine selection and reviews of only nineteen tasters, yet assumed the results were general fact rather than subjective taste.
